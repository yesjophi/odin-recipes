Steak

Description

A simple and easy meal that anyone can make

Ingredients

  • 195g slice of Sir Loin steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter (optional)
  • Neutral cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat pan on high heat.
  2. Add cooking oil to pan and allow it to heat up.
  3. Season steak with salt and pepper while waiting for pan to heat up. Ensure both sides and edges are covered in seasoning to taste.
  4. Sear the fat of the steak first for a few seconds (around 10 seconds should be sufficient) and then put it on its side. Allow steak to cook for roughly 2 minutes before flipping and repeating for another 2 minutes.
  5. Optional: Add butter and baste the steak for a few more seconds.
  6. Remove steak from heat and let it rest on a cutting board for a few minutes.
  7. Cut and serve.
  8. Enjoy!

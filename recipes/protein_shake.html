Protein Shake

Description

A simple and easy bevarage that even a dog can make for fast gains

Ingredients

  • Protein powder
  • Water or Milk

Steps

  1. Add 1 scoop of protein powder to your protein shaker
  2. Add 300-500ml of water/milk to your protein powder. Less water = thicker. More water = runnier and flavour is less potent
  3. Shake until all powder has dissolved
  4. Enjoy!
